ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Bailey Dalton.

I'm a web developer/designer from Las Vegas currently studying at Utah Valley University in Orem, Utah.

I started my education at BYU-Idaho where I took one introduction to web development and completely loved it. I later transferred to UVU where I have been able to continue my education in their web and app development program.

I have had the opportunity to work with a number of professors and fellow students who have helped and guided through my design and development projects. While I am passionate about design and development I am also very interested in project management and quality assurance. I plan on completing an internship after my junior year that will help me narrow down what I want to do in the future.

Ultimately, I know that I have a passion for people and relationships whether they be face to face or through a design and I want to be able to take that passion and apply it to my career.
